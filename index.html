About me

I'm a PhD Research Scientist at the University of Padua with six years of experience in diverse research projects.

My roles have included serving as a research unit member, work package leader, and project coordinator. The projects I've been involved with aimed to foster innovative partnerships between academia and private companies at European, national, and regional levels. My work focuses on optimizing resource use, driving sustainability initiatives, and assessing the environmental impact of sustainable production practices to facilitate the transition of farmers and private companies towards more sustainable production systems using innovative technologies and advanced data analysis tools.
